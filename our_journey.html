2018

The Beginning

It all started when our friend introduced me to you, even though I already knew and saw you before during my tutor days. We used to play together a lot as kids, and back then, I didn’t realize that you had a crush on me. But honestly, I already had a little clue because our friends would always tease us together.

2019

Fav Memory Back Then

One of my favorite memories with you was during Simbang Gabi. I still remember how unexpected it was that we were both chosen to be the alay that time. We didn’t even know that both of us were there until we saw each other, and honestly, it made that moment even more special for me.

2020

Pandemic Days

During this time, we lost contact with each other, but somehow we still managed to find our way back when we became classmates after I transferred to a new school. It felt nice seeing you again after so long, but after that school year ended, we slowly lost contact once more.

Dec 2021 - 2022

The Contact Is Back

Late that December, we found our way back to each other again. We were both upcoming Grade 8 students, even though we were already studying at different schools. This time, you finally confessed your feelings and started courting me. For a while, everything felt new and special, but sadly, things didn’t end the way we hoped. After a few months, we broke up and slowly lost contact once again.

2023 - 2024

Once Again

In November 2023, we found our way back to each other once again. Even after everything that happened before, we both still felt the same, so we decided to try again. You courted me again, and for a while, everything felt happy and right, like maybe this time things would finally work out for us. But after 10 months of courting, we ended up breaking apart again and lost contact once more.

2025

Tadhana

We found our way back to each other again, but this time, we both agreed that we would stay as just friends and nothing more. Back then, we both had our own crushes, but sadly, both of us ended up getting rejected. We became each other’s comfort person, listening to one another’s rants and sharing our feelings. That’s when I started thinking that maybe we were really meant for each other, because no matter how many times we lost contact, we always somehow found our way back. And this time, you courted me once again.

June 6, 2025

I Finally Said Yes

After a couple of months of you courting me, I finally said yes because I knew and trusted that you could take care of me and love me the way I deserved. You stayed patient with me through everything, and little by little, you made me feel safe, appreciated, and genuinely happy. For the first time in a long while, I felt certain about us. After all the times we lost each other and somehow found our way back, I realized that maybe this was finally our right time. And honestly, saying yes to you became one of the happiest decisions I’ve made.
